Research Interests

My research focuses on the structural dynamics of molecular machines using cryo-electron tomography and coarse-grained molecular dynamics. I am particularly interested in bacterial flagellar motors and their interactions within complex cellular environments.

Publications

  • Drobek, H., et al. (2024). High-resolution tomography of flagellar assembly. Cell.
  • Drobek, H., et al. (2023). MD simulations reveal mechanical gating mechanisms in motility complexes. Nature Communications.

Teaching & Mentoring

I have mentored undergraduate students in computational biology and served as a teaching assistant for graduate-level courses on biophysics and structural biology.
